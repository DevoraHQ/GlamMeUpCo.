About Me

Hello, and welcome to GLAM ME UP CO.

My name is Tijesuni, and I am the founder and creative designer behind GLAM ME UP CO. Fashion has always been more than just clothing to me. It is a form of self-expression, confidence, and individuality. As a bespoke fashion designer, I am passionate about creating unique garments that reflect the personality, style, and vision of each client.

Every design is carefully crafted with attention to detail, quality workmanship, and a commitment to excellence. From the initial concept and sketch to the final stitch, I take pride in creating pieces that are tailored to fit beautifully and make my clients feel confident and empowered.

At GLAM ME UP CO, I specialise in custom-made fashion, offering designs for special occasions, formal events, everyday elegance, and statement pieces that stand out. My goal is to transform ideas into wearable art, ensuring that every client receives a garment that is as unique as they are.

I believe that fashion should not always be one-size-fits-all. Every individual deserves clothing that celebrates their shape, personality, and lifestyle. Through bespoke design, I work closely with my clients to bring their vision to life while delivering exceptional quality and style.

Thank you for supporting GLAM ME UP CO. I look forward to helping you create a look that is truly your own.
